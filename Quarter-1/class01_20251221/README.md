### General vs. Custom Agents

- The "Smart Consultants" that live in your terminal
- "God Mode" over your OS to solve novel problems

**Custom Agents** (e.g., OpenAI SDK):

- The "Assembly Line" built for reliability, scale, and specific business workflows
